`NodeResizer`, `NodeResizeControl`, and `NodeToolbar` are now exported directly from `@vue-flow/core`, and the resize-control styles ship with `@vue-flow/core/dist/style.css`. The `@vue-flow/node-resizer` and `@vue-flow/node-toolbar` packages are deprecated; install only `@vue-flow/core` going forward.
